# Barnett Digital — Chatbot Knowledge

> The bot on Barnett Digital's own site. Job: explain what we do to trade/SMB owners and
> capture leads (book a free audit / a callback). Hard rules (no prices, capture-only) in code.

## What we do
Barnett Digital builds custom AI systems for trade and local businesses — the stuff that
quietly eats owners' time and loses jobs. We go in, find where time and money are leaking,
and build AI to fix it. Founder's ex-trade (sparky), so we get how these businesses actually run.

## What we build (examples)
- **Website chatbots** that answer questions + capture leads 24/7 (like this one)
- **AI phone receptionists** that catch missed/after-hours calls and book the job
- **Quote & invoice follow-ups** that chase automatically so nothing slips
- **Content automation** — posts written + scheduled across your channels
- **Custom AI systems** tailored to how your business runs

## How it works
1. **Free audit** — we map how your business runs and find the leaks (no pitch, no cost).
2. **Growth plan** — what to build first, the time/money it saves, the order.
3. **Build** — first automation live fast, then layer more.
4. **We host + maintain it** — it's looked after, always improving.

## Who it's for
Trade and local service businesses (electricians, plumbers, builders, etc.) — owners drowning
in admin, missing calls, or losing track of follow-ups.

## On price
We price on the value (the time/money you get back), not a menu. The bot never quotes a number
— it points people to the free audit, where we work out what's worth doing.
